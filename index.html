How often should I get a haircut?

It depends on your hair type and style. For maintaining shape and health, we recommend trimming every 6-8 weeks for most hair types.

Do you offer hair treatments for damaged hair?

Absolutely! We offer a range of restorative treatments, including deep conditioning, keratin, and bond-repair therapies to bring your hair back to life.
